Artists

Jinsang

Jinsang, an emerging American producer, is recognized for his smooth and nostalgic sound that effortlessly blends elements of hip-hop and chillwave.

His breakthrough project "Solitude" captivated listeners with dreamy instrumentals and laid-back rhythms, creating a serene listening experience.

With a focus on rich textures and soulful samples, Jinsang continues to carve his own path in the lo-fi music scene, inviting fans to unwind and reflect.

Kona Park

Lo-Fi Hip-Hop LP

2014

Kona Park is a tranquil blend of lo-fi beats and soothing melodies, creating a relaxing auditory experience that invites introspection.

This album captures the essence of serene moments, perfect for unwinding or reflecting on life's subtleties.

Jinsang's unique sound design and atmospheric textures transport listeners to a peaceful state, making it a perfect soundtrack for quiet afternoons.

About the artist

Jinsang is a talented producer and beatmaker known for his mellow, lo-fi hip-hop sound that blends elements of jazz and ambient music. Emerging from the underground music scene, Jinsang has garnered attention for his signature style, characterized by soothing melodies, smooth rhythms, and a nostalgic feel. His work often features samples from classic tracks, reimagined into laid-back beats that create an atmosphere perfect for studying or relaxation. Jinsang's ability to evoke emotion through simple yet effective compositions has made him a prominent figure in the lo-fi genre.



Beyond his musical contributions, Jinsang has built a dedicated online community, sharing insights into his creative process and offering production tutorials to aspiring artists. His engagement with fans through social media platforms has helped popularize the lo-fi movement, inspiring a new generation of musicians to explore the genre. Jinsang's work not only resonates with listeners but also emphasizes the importance of creativity and self-expression in music.
