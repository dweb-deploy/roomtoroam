Artists

Cavetown

Cavetown, an indie pop artist from the UK, is known for his heartfelt lyrics and catchy melodies, creating music that resonates deeply with his fans.

Since his rise to fame, Cavetown has captured the hearts of listeners with his intimate performances and relatable themes of love, self-discovery, and mental health.

His evolving discography showcases a unique blend of acoustic and electronic sounds, reflecting a genuine passion for storytelling through music.

Cavetown

Alternative/Indie LP

2015

"Cavetown" is a self-titled debut album by Cavetown that introduces listeners to his signature sound characterized by gentle melodies and heartfelt lyrics.

Its intimate production and relatable songwriting invite listeners to reflect on their own experiences, making it a perfect entry point into Cavetown's world.

Everything is Made of Stars

Alternative/Indie LP

2015

"Everything is Made of Stars" explores the complexities of life and relationships through Cavetown's signature lens of introspection and whimsy.

With infectious hooks and relatable lyrics, this album captures the essence of navigating life's ups and downs, resonating with a broad audience.

Lemon Boy

Alternative/Indie LP

2018

"Lemon Boy" is a breakthrough album that catapulted Cavetown into the spotlight, showcasing his knack for blending whimsical storytelling with poignant reflections on mental health and self-acceptance.

With its eclectic mix of acoustic and electronic elements, the album creates a vibrant atmosphere which encapsulates the essence of embracing one’s quirks and the struggles of growing up.

Sleepyhead

Alternative/Indie EP

2020

The "Sleepyhead" EP features a collection of Cavetown's early work, highlighting his unique style of blending soft vocals with intricate guitar work.

With its charming melodies and heartfelt lyrics, this EP offers a glimpse into Cavetown's evolving artistry and sets the stage for his future projects.

Worm Food

Alternative/Indie LP

2022

"Worm Food" serves as a poignant exploration of mortality and self-discovery, wrapped in Cavetown's characteristic blend of humor and sensitivity.

With lush arrangements and thoughtful lyrics, this album dives deep into personal narratives, addressing the inevitability of change while offering moments of light-heartedness amidst the serious themes.

About the artist

Cavetown, an indie pop artist from the UK, has garnered a dedicated following with his introspective lyrics and catchy melodies. He began by sharing his music on platforms like YouTube and SoundCloud, where his heartfelt songs struck a chord with listeners worldwide. His breakthrough album, "Cavetown," released in 2015, showcased his unique blend of acoustic and electronic sounds, reflecting themes of love, mental health, and self-acceptance. Over the years, Cavetown has continued to evolve as an artist, collaborating with various musicians and expanding his sound.



Cavetown's ability to connect with his audience on a personal level has made his music resonate deeply, often addressing struggles with identity and belonging. He often incorporates his experiences into his songwriting, creating an intimate atmosphere that draws listeners in. Following the success of "Lemon Boy," he released several singles and EPs that further explored his evolving sound, incorporating elements of folk and pop. His thoughtful approach to songwriting and relatable themes have established him as a voice for a generation seeking authenticity and emotional resonance in music.
