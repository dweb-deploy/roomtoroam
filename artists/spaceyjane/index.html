Artists

Spacey Jane

Spacey Jane, an Australian indie rock band, is celebrated for their infectious melodies and honest storytelling that connect deeply with listeners.

Rising to prominence with their debut EP, the band captured attention with their vibrant guitar-driven sound and introspective lyrics.

Their ability to craft songs that balance lighthearted energy with raw emotion has solidified their place in the indie music scene.

No Way to Treat an Animal

Indie Pop/Garage Rock EP

2017

No Way to Treat an Animal is Spacey Jane's debut EP, a striking introduction that blends alternative rock with reflective lyrics and catchy melodies.

The EP captures the complexities of youth, exploring themes of anxiety, relationships, and the search for meaning through vibrant instrumentation.

With its infectious hooks and honest storytelling, this record invites listeners to connect with their own experiences while enjoying a fresh sound.

In the Meantime

Indie Rock LP

2018

In the Meantime marks a significant evolution for Spacey Jane, presenting a compelling narrative that intertwines personal reflection with broader themes of existence.

This collection features a diverse range of tracks that delve into personal struggles and the nuances of everyday life, all wrapped in captivating melodies.

This album encapsulates the band's ability to translate everyday experiences into relatable anthems, creating a rich tapestry of sound that lingers long after the last note fades.

In the Slight

Indie Rock/Folk-Pop LP

2018

In the Slight showcases Spacey Jane's knack for blending upbeat instrumentation with profound lyrical depth.

The album explores the complexities of relationships and the ebb and flow of emotions, inviting listeners to reflect on their own experiences through vivid storytelling.

With its vibrant soundscapes and playful yet poignant melodies, In the Slight encourages a sense of liberation, celebrating the nuances of life's fleeting moments.

Sunlight

Indie/Alternative Rock LP

2020

Sunlight radiates warmth and optimism, offering a refreshing perspective on life's challenges through its infectious melodies.

This album captures the essence of youthful exuberance while addressing themes of growth, resilience, and the pursuit of happiness.

Sunlight creates an uplifting atmosphere that encourages listeners to embrace their own journeys with hope and joy.

Here Comes Everybody

Indie/Alternative Rock LP

2022

Here Comes Everybody unfolds like a vibrant tapestry of sound, where each track weaves together the complexities of human connection and the challenges of modern life.

This album delves into themes of belonging and identity, reflecting the band's evolution while maintaining their signature charm.

Here Comes Everybody explores the intricacies of individual experiences within a shared community.

About the artist

Formed in Perth in 2016, the band consists of Caleb Harper (vocals, guitar), Ashton Hardman-Le Cornu (guitar), Kieran Lama (drums), and Peppa Lane (bass). Spacey Jane quickly gained traction with their debut EP, *No Way to Treat an Animal*, showcasing their knack for blending catchy melodies with relatable lyrics. Their music combines elements of alternative rock, pop, and grunge, characterized by jangly guitars, driving rhythms, and Caleb's distinctive vocals. The band's engaging sound and authentic storytelling have resonated deeply with audiences, making them a prominent name in the Australian music scene.



Spacey Jane's lyrics often explore themes of anxiety, relationships, and self-discovery, inviting listeners to connect with their experiences. Their most recent album, "Here Comes Everybody", further solidified their place in the indie rock landscape, featuring an evolution in their sound while maintaining their signature charm. With a growing fanbase and critically acclaimed releases, Spacey Jane continues to make waves in the music industry, offering fresh and relatable music that speaks to a new generation of listeners.
