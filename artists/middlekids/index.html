About the artist

Middle Kids are an Australian indie rock band known for their heartfelt lyrics, infectious melodies, and dynamic sound. Formed in Sydney in 2016, the band consists of lead vocalist and guitarist Hannah Joy, bassist Tim Fitz, and drummer Harry Day. Their music blends indie rock with folk and pop influences, creating songs that balance raw emotion with polished instrumentation. Middle Kids quickly rose to prominence with their hit single "Edge of Town," which earned widespread acclaim and set the stage for their critically praised breakthrough album, Lost Friends.



Middle Kids' music often delves into themes of vulnerability, personal growth, and relationships, resonating with listeners through its emotional honesty. The band's knack for crafting anthemic choruses paired with introspective lyrics has set them apart in the indie scene. Their distinct sound, marked by Hannah Joy's powerful vocals and the band's tight, cohesive arrangements, continues to captivate fans and critics alike, solidifying their place as a standout act in modern indie rock.
