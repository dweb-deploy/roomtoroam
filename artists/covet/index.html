Covet

Covet, a captivating American instrumental artist, is celebrated for their lush soundscapes and intricate guitar melodies that evoke deep emotions.

They gained attention with their breakthrough album "Effloresce," enchanting listeners with a unique fusion of math rock and ambient textures.

With a focus on collaboration, Covet often partners with various artists, enriching their sound and expanding their creative horizons.

About the artist

Covet is an instrumental progressive rock band hailing from San Jose, renowned for their intricate melodies and captivating soundscapes. Formed in 2014 by guitarist Yvette Young, Covet quickly gained a following with their distinctive blend of math rock and post-rock influences, characterized by Yvette's virtuosic guitar work, lush textures, and emotive compositions. The band's music often explores themes of introspection and personal growth, with dynamic shifts that create an immersive listening experience.



In addition to their musical achievements, Covet is recognized for their commitment to inclusivity and fostering community within the music scene. Yvette Young, a prominent figure in the guitar community, actively engages with fans and aspiring musicians through social media, sharing insights into her creative process and promoting self-expression. The band's dedication to creating a positive environment has not only inspired their audience but has also helped elevate the conversation around diversity and representation in the music industry.
