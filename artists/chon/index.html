Chon

Chon, an American progressive rock band renowned for their intricate guitar work and dynamic compositions, has carved out a unique niche in the instrumental music world.

With their debut album "Grow," they enchanted listeners through a vibrant mix of complex rhythms and melodic interplay, earning a dedicated following.

Their evolving sound blends elements of jazz, math rock, and post-rock, showcasing their creativity and passion for musical innovation.

About the artist

Chon is a progressive rock band from Oceanside, California, known for their intricate, math rock-inspired sound and impressive musicianship. Formed in 2008, the band consists of Mario Camarena (guitar), Erick Hansel (guitar), and Nathan Camarena (drums). Drawing influences from jazz, metal, and post-rock, Chon’s music is characterized by its complex rhythms, rapid time signature changes, and smooth transitions between mellow melodies and high-energy riffs. Their instrumental approach, free of vocals, allows their musicality to shine and creates a sound that is both intricate and accessible.



Chon has headlined tours internationally and performed at major festivals, earning a loyal fanbase drawn to their unique style and live performances. Chon's music has continued to evolve, maintaining their signature sound while exploring new sonic landscapes, solidifying their place as one of the most innovative acts in modern instrumental rock.
