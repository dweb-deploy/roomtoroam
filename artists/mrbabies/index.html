Artists

Mr Babies

Mr Babies is a multidisciplinary artist whose work blends surrealism with vibrant colors and playful, dreamlike imagery.

Emerging as a prominent figure in contemporary art, he has gained recognition for his unique ability to combine humor and fantasy in visually striking pieces.

Mr Babies continues to evolve his creative practice, exploring themes of imagination and nostalgia, offering viewers a whimsical escape from reality.

2021

Mermaid Manifestations by Mr. Babies showcases his mastery of surreal collage art, blending vibrant imagery with psychedelic undertones. Known for his analog technique, Mr. Babies (Nick Baccari) uses physical materials like magazines to create intricate compositions that defy conventional expectations. This particular piece features an amalgamation of aquatic themes intertwined with human elements, highlighting his signature style of combining organic forms with surreal and fantastical narratives.

Split Personality

Magazine Clipping Collage

2022

Split Personality by Mr. Babies continues his exploration of surrealism through collage, blending disjointed elements to create a fragmented yet cohesive narrative. The piece features a striking juxtaposition of human faces and forms, layered and intertwined to evoke the concept of duality. Each fragment represents a different emotion or facet of identity, capturing the essence of conflicting inner states.

The layering of natural landscapes with mythical elements showcases Mr. Babies' ability to merge the familiar with the fantastical, drawing viewers into a dreamlike journey.

About the artist

Mr Babies is an enigmatic and experimental artist known for blending surrealism with digital art and illustration. His work often features dreamlike landscapes, peculiar characters, and vibrant colors that challenge conventional boundaries of visual storytelling. With a distinct style that evokes a sense of whimsy and the bizarre, Mr Babies explores themes of identity, fantasy, and the subconscious. His digital creations have garnered a following online, where he continues to push the limits of imagination, creating artwork that feels both familiar and otherworldly.



Mr Babies' work spans a variety of mediums, including digital prints, animated short films, and multimedia installations. His ability to evoke emotion through abstract and often surreal imagery has resonated with art lovers and collectors alike. As a rising figure in the digital art space, Mr Babies continues to innovate, drawing inspiration from both contemporary culture and the surrealist movement. His art invites viewers to immerse themselves in strange, captivating worlds, leaving lasting impressions that encourage deeper exploration of the human psyche and imagination.
